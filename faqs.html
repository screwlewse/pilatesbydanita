WHAT IS PILATES?

Balance, Tone, Relax, and Energizing


"The Pilates method of body conditioning is a unique system of stretching and strengthening exercises developed over ninety years ago by Joseph Pilates. It strengthens and tones muscles, improves posture, provides flexibility and balance, unites the body and mind, and creates a more streamlined shape."
Brooke Siler
The Pilates Body

Stott Pilates is one of the leaders in a contemporary approach to Pilates today. This exercise system is a set of non-impact exercises that focus on the deep muscles of the abdomen and spine. Pilates exercises teach awareness of neutral alignment of the spine. Designed to stretch and strengthen, thereby creating long, lean muscles without bulk, these activities are composed of many controlled, precise movements.

Pilates requires concentration and deep, controlled breaths for a complete mind-body workout. Pilates is a lifelong journey that will strengthen, lengthen, balance, tone, relax, and energize you.
