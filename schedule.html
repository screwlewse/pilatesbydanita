CLASS SCHEDULE

These classes are what I currently provide, and new classes are forming weekly!
Day / Time Class Name Info
Saturdays 9 AM Spinning Interval Class

Take out the intimidation of a big gym spinning class. This spin class is the place to begin to learn how to spin correctly and safely. You will be fitted to the bike properly, taught the correct and safe form and have an enjoyable ride to some awesome tunes. We will spend part of the time on the bike and part off the bike building strength in our upper body as well. The most perfect blend of cardio and strength training!
Tuesdays 6:30 PM Beginning Mat Class

A class for those beginning their journey in Pilates. You will learn the basics with much attention to detail. Limited to only 4-5 people.
