PILATES BY DANITA

A Place for Wellness, Strength, and Flexibility

Established in 2005 in San Leandro, California, and re-located to the Washington DC area, Pilates By Danita is a fully equipped, state of the art, Pilates and personal training studio. Fully furnished with Stott Pilates superior equipment, Pilates By Danita offers training on all the apparatus including the Reformer, Stability Chair, Cadillac (Trapeze Table), and all Barrels. Small group classes in Pilates, fitness and aerobics are enhanced by incorporating the use of resistance bands, Pilates fitness circles, foam rollers, various stability balls, TRX Suspension Training and BOSU Balance Trainers.

THE MISSION

Creating Passion for Wellness

Pilates By Danita’s mission is to create a place where you can learn, focus and challenge yourself physically resulting in a passion for your own wellness. This investment in yourself will continue into your everyday life and activities. Whether you are a novice, pained body, young or not so young, Pilates By Danita works towards creating a motivating and encouraging environment where you can be inspired to wellness.
